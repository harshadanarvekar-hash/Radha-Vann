Our Story

Radha Vann started from a very personal place. Like every parent, we think deeply about what goes into our child’s body. Food is not just about taste — it is about health, growth, and care.

If we are so careful about what we feed our own child, why shouldn’t the same care go into the food we grow for others? That thought became Radha Vann.

We grow our food ourselves so there are no shortcuts and no compromises. We know the soil, the seasons, and how each crop is nurtured. Farming for us is not business first — it is responsibility.

If it’s not good enough for our child, it doesn’t go out.
