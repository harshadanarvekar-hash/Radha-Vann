About Us

About Us – Radha Vann Radha Vann started from a very personal place. Like every parent, we think deeply about what goes into our child’s body. What they eat matters because food is not just about taste; it’s about health, growth, and care. One simple question stayed with us: If we are so careful about what we feed our own child, why shouldn’t the same care go into the food we grow for others? That thought became Radha Vann. We grow our food ourselves, so there is no guessing, no shortcuts, and no compromises. We know the soil, the water, and the way each crop is nurtured, because we are involved at every step. Everything is grown with patience and responsibility, just the way we would grow food for our own family. For us, farming is not a business first; it is a responsibility. The produce that leaves Radha Vann is the same food we bring into our own kitchen. If it’s not good enough for our child, it doesn’t go out. Radha Vann is our way of sharing that care and honesty with you simple, clean food grown with love, the way it should be.
